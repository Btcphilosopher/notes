NOTICE REGARDING DEBANKING AND SUSPENSION OF FINANCIAL SERVICES ACCESS

From Thomas Samuel Loveitt,

To all relevant financial institutions, service providers, payment processors, and counterparties with whom I have held or attempted to hold accounts, facilities, or transactional arrangements,

It is hereby acknowledged and formally recorded that access to certain banking and financial services associated with my name has been restricted, suspended, or withdrawn, including but not limited to account facilities, payment processing capabilities, and any forms of credit or transactional privilege previously made available.

This notice is not issued as an allegation of misconduct toward any individual or institution, nor does it assert wrongdoing on either side. It is instead a statement of fact regarding the present unavailability of standard financial services and the effective discontinuation of banking access that would ordinarily permit the receipt, storage, or transfer of funds through conventional channels.

Accordingly, I recognise that I am presently unable to rely upon standard banking infrastructure for the conduct of routine financial activity. Any obligations, receipts, or transactions must therefore be managed through alternative means where available, including direct transfer methods, cash-based arrangements, or other mutually agreed forms of settlement.

This situation is noted without prejudice to any prior good-faith dealings, and without waiving any rights of review, appeal, or reconsideration that may exist under applicable processes or regulatory frameworks. Any future restoration of services shall be contingent upon reassessment by the relevant institutions and compliance with their prevailing requirements.

This statement is issued for clarity of record and personal reference.
